Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Our raffle management application uses Google AdMob to display advertising. For this purpose, device identifiers (Ad ID) may be collected for ad personalization.

2. User Consent (UMP)

In accordance with applicable regulations and international standards, we request your prior consent for the processing of data for advertising purposes through Google's official SDK.

3. Local Data Storage

The application stores raffle data and associated images locally on your device. Selected images may be cropped before being saved. Additionally, configuration preferences (such as visual theme) are stored. This information is not transmitted to external servers.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Local data (raffles, images, and preferences) remains on your device until you manually delete it from the application or uninstall it. When you delete a raffle, its associated images are also removed. When you uninstall the application, all data is automatically deleted from your device.

5. Security

Application data is stored in the device's private internal storage, which is not accessible by other applications. Images are compressed in WebP format to optimize storage usage.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data at any time from within the application.
  • Revoke advertising consent from the app settings or your Android device settings.
  • Request information about the processing of your data by contacting us through the means indicated below.

7. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since all data is stored locally on the device, a parent or guardian can delete it at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing its data from the device settings. The advertising identifier (Ad ID) is managed by Google and can be disabled from Android's privacy settings.
